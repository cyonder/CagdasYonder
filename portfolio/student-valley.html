Problem

During my education in college I was frustrated with use of Blackboard, which is a "Learning Management System" that is used by many colleges and universities all around the world. This system provides a platform for students to sell their old books, message other students, ask questions and many other features like this. But because of its very bad design only a few students were using it. Eventually, I decided to start a project as an alternative to Blackboard.

Solution

I believe that essential to design is the ability to communicate and publish. Part of the mission of Student Valley is for everyone to become a publisher. This site is created to encourage communication, sharing, helping, and publishing.

Process
